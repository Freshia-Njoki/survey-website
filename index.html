About Us

Geomtec Geomatics Limited (GGL) brings a fresh and innovative approach to consulting services, in the field of
surveying for planning and execution of civil engineering schemes. Backed by highly trained and experienced
personnel in this field, the company is the force behind successful surveys for feasibility studies, designs and
implementation of major engineering projects in Kenya and the wider East and Central Africa region.


Our goal is to exceed the expectations of every client by offering outstanding customer service, increased
flexibility, and greater value, thus optimizing and improving operation efficiency. Our associates are distinguished
by their functional and technical expertise combined with their hands-on experience, thereby eginsuring that our
clients receive the most effective and professional service. The company is incorporated in Kenya, with the
registered office in the capital city, Nairobi. It is 100% locally owned; and is run by a board of three directors.
